# Career Document Templates

This resource provides templates for academic career documents.

---

## Research Statement Template

### Opening Paragraph

```markdown
[HOOK: Compelling statement of your research focus]
[My field] is at an exciting juncture because [why this moment matters].

[BIG PICTURE: Why this matters]
Understanding [your focus area] is critical because [significance to field/society].

[YOUR CONTRIBUTION: Position yourself]
My research addresses this challenge by [your approach/contribution]. I have
[key accomplishment] and [another accomplishment], positioning me to [future impact].
```

### Past Research Section

```markdown
## Research Accomplishments

### [Theme 1: Descriptive Title]

[Motivation for this research direction]

My work on [topic] has [key contribution]. Specifically, I [what you did], which
resulted in [outcome - publications, citations, methods adopted]. This work was
published in [journals] and has been cited [N] times / adopted by [N] labs.

[Your specific role if collaborative]
In this work, I independently developed [your contribution], while collaborating
with [collaborators] on [their contribution].

### [Theme 2: Descriptive Title]

[Continue same structure]

### [Theme 3: Descriptive Title]

[Continue same structure]
```

### Future Research Section

```markdown
## Future Research Directions

My future research will build on these foundations to address [overarching goal].
I will pursue three interconnected directions:

### Direction 1: [Descriptive Title]

**The Question:** [What you will investigate]

**Why It Matters:** [Significance]

**The Approach:** I will [methods/approach]. This builds on my expertise in [past work]
and leverages [resources at institution if applicable].

**Expected Impact:** This work will [outcomes and significance].

**Funding Strategy:** This direction is well-suited for [NIH institute / NSF program /
foundation] funding.

### Direction 2: [Descriptive Title]

[Same structure]

### Direction 3: [Descriptive Title]

[Same structure]
```

### Closing Paragraph

```markdown
## Vision and Fit

My research program combines [strengths] to address [big question]. At [Institution],
I would benefit from [specific resources/collaborators], and I would contribute to
the department's strengths in [area].

In summary, my work will [impact statement]. I am excited to build a research
program that [vision] while training the next generation of [scientists/scholars].
```
